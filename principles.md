# Principles — non-negotiables

1. Ship beats perfect. A live page with one product > a flawless plan.
2. Every action either generates demand or closes a sale. If neither, kill it.
3. Customer data is sacred. Never expose, commit, or share it.
4. One piece at a time. Draft → review → ship → next.
5. Codify repetition. 3rd time doing a thing → it becomes a skill.
6. No surprises with money or deletes. Confirm before spending or removing.
